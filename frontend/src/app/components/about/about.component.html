Our Story

☕

Born from a love of great coffee and meaningful conversations, The Haven opened its doors in 2018 in the heart of the city. What started as a small corner café with a single espresso machine has blossomed into a beloved gathering spot for coffee lovers, creatives, and dreamers.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms across Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, roasting them in small batches to unlock their fullest flavor. Every cup is a journey — from the soil to your soul.

Beyond coffee, we take pride in our house-made pastries, artisan sandwiches, and a rotating seasonal menu that celebrates local ingredients.
